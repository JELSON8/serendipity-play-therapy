What is Play Therapy?

Play is how children naturally communicate. Play therapy uses that language to help children understand and express what they are feeling — at their own pace, in a safe and trusting space.

🧸

Their Natural Language

Children explore their feelings through play, the way that comes most naturally to them.

💛

A Safe Space

A gentle, accepting environment where every child feels heard and understood.

🌱

Room to Grow

Building confidence, resilience and emotional wellbeing, one session at a time.

Who Can It Help?

Play therapy can be beneficial to all children, and is especially helpful for those who have experienced:

Trauma Bereavement Family breakdown Anxiety Anger Abuse Social difficulties Emotional difficulties
